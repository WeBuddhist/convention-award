# Buddhist Creators Convention 2027 & Award 

# Overview 

BCC brings together Buddhist content creators and equips them to share the Dharma skillfully across digital platforms. Our goal is to build a professional global community of Buddhist creators with a collective reach of 100 million followers by 2030. 

### 📋 Planning Workstreams
 
### 🗓️ Event Logistics & Coordination
- Venue research, shortlisting, and confirmation (Bodhgaya, TBC)
- 4–5 day convention format (refined from 2026's 9-day structure)
- Schedule and run-of-show planning
- On-ground team roles with clear responsibilities and minimal overlap
- Dedicated coordinator for oversight, delegation, and problem-solving
- Participant observer assigned to monitor experience in real time
- Walkie-talkie coordination continued from 2026

### 📝 Participant Selection & Registration
- Structured application platform with motivation letter requirement
- Application fee introduced (variable by country, e.g. 1,000–5,000 INR)
- Formal selection committee with defined criteria
- Attendance and certificate eligibility tracking
- No free accommodation — participants arrange and cover their own stay
- Alumni and volunteer pipeline from BCC 2026

### 🎤 Convention Programming
- **Opening ceremony**
- **Panel discussion**
- **Lightning Talks** / Creator Showcase across four concurrent tracks 
- **Community Circles**: problem-based thematic group discussions
- Dedicated meditation sessions and short Dharma teachings (new for 2027)
- **Special guest teacher session**
- Recap and consolidation session
- **Gala dinner and closing ceremony**

### 🏆 Buddhist Creators Awards 2027
- Award categories confirmed and communicated early
- Nominations open to convention participants and broader community
- Real-time audience voting via Mentimeter
- Stronger ceremony production: storytelling, visuals, and advance winner preparation
- Post-award social media amplification integrated with WeBuddhist app

  **Award Categories:**
- **Buddhist Influencer of the Year** — consistent global reach, authenticity, and innovation
- **Rising Star Award** — most remarkable growth and potential
- **Audience Choice Award** — participant-voted from convention-created videos
- **Most Creative Video Award** — creativity, storytelling, and technical excellence
- **Most Followers Gained** — highest follower growth during the convention

### 📣 Outreach & Marketing
- Year-round social media presence (not only during event period)
- Testimonial videos and content from BCC 2026 participants and award winners
- Multilingual materials: English, Hindi, Tibetan, Nepali
- Sponsor and partner communications
- WeBuddhist app promotion integrated throughout all channels
 
### 💰 Budget & Financial Planning
- Detailed budget defined from the start of planning
- All elements clearly costed: venue, production, logistics, awards, materials
- Application fee revenue factored in
- Vision Partner contributions tracked separately

- ## 🎯 Milestones
 
- **Phase 1 — Foundation:** Venue shortlist, team roles confirmed, budget drafted, selection committee formed
- **Phase 2 — Partnerships:** Vision Partner outreach begins, tiers and benefits confirmed, agreements signed
- **Phase 3 — Applications:** Application platform live, fee structure set, selection criteria defined
- **Phase 4 — Programming:** Convention schedule finalized, speakers confirmed, award categories announced
- **Phase 5 — Outreach:** Registration open, year-round social media active, multilingual materials ready
- **Phase 6 — Execution:** Convention delivered, awards ceremony
- **Phase 7 — Post-Convention:** Alumni engagement, localized masterclass rollout, partner impact report, BCC 2028 debrief

- ## 👥 Stakeholders
 
- **WeBuddhist / Dharmaduta Services Team:** Core organizers and project leads
- **Masterclass Coaches:** Faculty for educational programming
- **Vision Partners:** Financial backers, product donors, and collaborating organizations
- **Community Volunteers:** On-ground and remote support; BCC 2026 alumni prioritized
- **WeBuddhist App Team:** App integration throughout convention experience
